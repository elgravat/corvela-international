Who We Serve

Your Independent Health Insurance Broker

Corvela International is an independent health insurance brokerage built for people who live, work, and travel across borders. We help travelers, expats, and digital nomads find the right international health plan — without the confusion, the jargon, or the pressure.

We're not tied to any single carrier. We compare options across leading providers, explain the trade-offs honestly, and recommend what actually fits your situation and budget. And because carriers pay us directly, our guidance costs you nothing.

Plans for Every Situation

Whether you're away for two weeks or two years, there's a plan designed for your situation. We'll help you find it.

Travel Health Insurance

Short-term coverage for trips lasting days to months. Emergency care, hospitalization, and medical evacuation included from day one.

Expat Health Plans

Comprehensive, annually renewable coverage for those living outside their home country. Designed to be your primary health insurance abroad.

Digital Nomad Coverage

Portable, flexible plans that follow you across countries without gaps. No fixed address required. Coverage stays active as you move.
